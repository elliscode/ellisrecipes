# Snow Sugar

## Ingredients

- `45` g (`1/4` cup) powdered sugar
- `5` g (`1.5` tsp) potato starch

## Directions

- add to a bowl and whisk to combine
- add to baking sifter and apply to desserts

Servings: 2

Category: Desserts
