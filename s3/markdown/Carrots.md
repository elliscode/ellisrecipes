# Carrots

## Equipment

- Pot with lid
- Steamer basket
- Magic bullet blender

## Ingredients

- `20` baby carrots
- Water

## Instructions

- Heat water in pot until boiling
- Add steamer basket filled with carrots and steam for 15 minutes
- Add to blender cup and add cooking water until it blends into a smooth paste

Servings: 4
