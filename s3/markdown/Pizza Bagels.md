# Pizza Bagels

## Ingredients

- `1/2` bagel
- `35` g pizza gravy
- `1/2` oz cheese
- `4` slices pepperoni

## Directions

- preheat oven to 425F
- place assembled pizza bagels on a tray or a pizza stone and bake for 13 minutes, or until cheese is toasty
- let cool for 5 minutes before eating to avoid burning your face off
