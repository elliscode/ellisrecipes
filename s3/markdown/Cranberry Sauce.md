# Cranberry Sauce

## Ingredients

- `12` oz cranberries, washed and rinsed
- `200` g white sugar
- `1` cup orange juice

## Directions

- Add all ingredients to a pot
- bring to a boil
- reduce to a simmer and cook for 10 min
- let cool for two hours before putting in the fridge
