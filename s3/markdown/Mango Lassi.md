# Mango Lassi

## Ingredients

- `1.5` cup canned alphonso pulp
- `175` g cold plain yogurt
- `3/4` cup cold milk
- `1.5` TBSP sugar
- `1/4` tsp cardamom powder

## Directions

- add everything to a blender
- blend until smooth
- refrigerate for an hour
- serve 

Servings: 4
