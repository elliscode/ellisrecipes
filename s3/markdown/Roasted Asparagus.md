# Roasted Asparagus

## Ingredients

- 1 large bunch (about 1 pound) fresh asparagus
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 1/4 tsp salt, to taste
- Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- 1/2 lemons juice
- 1/2 lemons wedges
- 1 TBSP parmesan cheese

## Directions

- preheat to 425
- roast thin asparagus for 10 min, thick for 20 min
