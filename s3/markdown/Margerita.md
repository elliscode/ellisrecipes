# Margerita

## Ingredients

- Deselect All
- Ice cubes
- `3` oz tequila
- `2` oz freshly squeezed lime juice
- `1` oz Simple Syrup, recipe follows
- `1/2` to `1` tsp orange liqueur
- `1` TBSP Lime-salt-sugar, recipe follows

### Simple Syrup:

- `12.5` g sugar
- `14.7` g water

### Lime-salt-sugar:

- Zest of one lime
- `2` TBSP kosher salt
- `2` TBSP sugar

Servings: 1
