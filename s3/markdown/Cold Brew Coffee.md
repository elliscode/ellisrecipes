# Cold Brew Coffee

## Ingredients

- `3` cups water
- `3/4` cup coarse ground coffee

## Directions

- Combine coarse ground coffee and water in a mason
- Place in fridge and let steep overnight
- Run through a coffee filter in a pourover the next day
