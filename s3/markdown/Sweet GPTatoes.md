# Sweet GPTatoes

## Ingredients

### Sweet Potatoes

- 3-4 medium sweet potatoes, peeled + cut into thick cubes
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)

### Hot Honey Brown Butter

- 4 tbsp unsalted butter
- 11/2 tbsp honey (or maple syrup)
- 1-2 tsp hot sauce OR pinch of cayenne
- 1/2 tsp apple cider vinegar (key!)

### Garlic Herb Crunch

- 2 tbsp butter OR olive oil
- 3-4 garlic cloves, finely minced
- 1/3 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 1 tbsp chopped rosemary
- 1 tbsp chopped thyme
- Pinch of salt

## Steps

### Roast the sweet potatoes

- Preheat oven to 425F.
- Toss cubes with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
- Spread onto a baking sheet without crowding.
- Roast 30-40 minutes, turning once, until edges are crispy and brown.

### Make the Hot Honey Brown Butter

- In a small pan, melt the butter until it starts to foam and turn golden brown (nutty smell).
- Remove from heat, whisk in honey, hot sauce/cayenne, and vinegar.
- Taste - should be sweet-spicy-tangy. Adjust heat or sweetness as needed.

### Make the Garlic Herb Crunch

- Add butter/oil to a pan over medium heat.
- Add garlic; cook 10-20 seconds (don't brown).
- Add panko and herbs.
- Toast until golden and crispy. Salt lightly.

### Assemble

- Transfer roasted sweet potatoes to a serving dish.
- Drizzle with hot honey brown butter.
- Shower generously with the garlic herb crunch.
- Add a last sprinkle of flaky salt.
