# Greek Seasoning
## Ingredients
- `3` tablespoons oregano
- `2` tablespoons basil
- `1` tablespoon dill
- `2` tablespoons onion powder
- `2` tablespoons garlic powder
- `1/2` tablespoon salt
- `1` tablespoon black pepper

Servings: 16
