# Pasta and SDTs

## Ingredients

### Chicken 

- `1.5` lb raw chicken breast, fileted and cut into small pieces
- `1` TBSP olive oil
- `1/2` tsp salt
- `1/4` tsp pepper

### Pasta Sauce

- `1` cup sun-dried tomatoes packed in oil, drained and diced
- `1/4` tsp salt
- `1/4` tsp black pepper
- `1/4` tsp garlic powder
- `1/2` tsp oregano
- `1/4` tsp dried basil
- `1/8` tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
- `2` TBSP tomato paste
- `2` cups chicken broth
- `1/2` cup heavy cream
- `1/2` cup of chopped baby spinach
- `1` oz. Parmesan, finely grated (about `1/2` cup), plus more for serving

### Pasta

- `1` lb of whatever kind of pasta you want

## Directions

### Pasta

- boil a big ol pot of water
- add a handful of salt
- boil the pasta to al dente directions on the bag
- drain and coat in olive oil in the strainer so it doesnt stick together

### Chicken

- while waiting for the water to boil, heat oil in a pan
- add the raw chicken and salt and pepper, and cook until its cooked
- set aside when its done, we'll add this to the sauce later

### Sauce

- add the sun dried tomatoes and spices to a pan and saute for 2 min
- add the tomato paste, broth, and cream and bring to a boil
- reduce heat and add the pasta, chicken, baby spinach, and parmesan
