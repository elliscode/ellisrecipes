# Peanut Sauce
## Ingredients
- 4 oz water
- 4 g corn starch
- 6 g better than bouillon no beef
- 35 g soy sauce
- 2 g sesame oil
- 2 g sriracha
- 9 g rice wine vinegar
- 33 g peanut Butter
- 13 g brown sugar
- 1/8 tsp ground ginger
## Directions
- mix all ingredients together in a wok over medium heat until sauce is uniform and thick

Servings: 2
