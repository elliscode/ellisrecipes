# Bacon Lettuce Tomato

## Equipment

- cast iron skillet
- toaster

## Ingredients

- `8` oz bacon
- `4` slices bread
- romaine lettuce
- cherry tomatoes
- red onions
- mayo

## Directions

- put cast iron skillet over low heat for 10 mins
- add bacon in batches and cook for 20 minutes until crispy
- assemble sandwich

Servings: 2

Category: Sandwiches
