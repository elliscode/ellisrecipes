# Balsamic Vinaigrette

## Ingredients
- 45 g olive oil
- 18 g balsamic vinegar
- 11 g honey
- 4 g mustard
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp ground pepper

## Directions
- combine all ingredients in bowl
- whisk to emulsify

Servings: 2
