# Teriyaki Sauce
## Ingredients
- 4 oz water
- 1 TBSP cornstarch
- 6 g better than bouillon no chicken
- 30 g packed brown sugar
- 35 g soy sauce
- 10 g honey honey
- 1/8 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp ground ginger
## Directions
- Whisk together all ingredients in a small bowl
- Heat in wok until thickened

Servings: 2
