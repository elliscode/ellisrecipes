# Buttered Couscous
## Ingredients
- 8 oz water
- 1 TBSP butter
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 186 g couscous
## Directions
- bring water, butter, and 1/2 tsp salt to a boil
- add couscous and stir, remove from heat, then cover and wait 5 mins
- fluff couscous and serve
Servings: 2
