# Greek Pasta

## Ingredients

- 1/4 recipe homemade san marzano pasta sauce
- 1/8 tsp cinnamon
- 2 oz feta, crumbled
- 6 oz pasta, cooked and drained

## Directions

- add cinnamon to pasta sauce
- combine pasta, sauce, and feta, and stir in a skillet to combine
- serve

Servings: 2
