# Lemon Couscous
## Ingredients
- 8 oz water
- 8 g better than bouillon NO chicken
- 2 TBSP lemon juice
- 1/2 TBSP butter
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 4 oz jar diced pimento peppers
- 15 g pine nuts
- 2 tsp dried parsley
- 186 g couscous
## Directions
- add everything but couscous to a small pot
- bring to a boil
- add couscous and stir
- remove from heat, cover and wait five minutes
- fluff and serve
Servings: 2
