# Oatmeal Smoothie
## Ingredients
- 1/4 cup oats
- 1 banana
- 1/2 cup plant milk
- 1 TBSP peanut butter
- 1 tsp maple syrup
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/8 tsp salt
## Directions
- Combine all ingredients in a blender
Servings: 1
