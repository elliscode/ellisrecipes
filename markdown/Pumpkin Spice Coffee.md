# Pumpkin Spice Coffee
## Ingredients
- 15 g coffee
- 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
## Directions
- Add coffee and pumpkin pie spice to a coffee filter
- Pour over 8 oz of 200F water

Servings: 1
