# Pastina

## Ingredients

- water
- salt
- 56 g pastina
- 7 g butter

## Directions

- bring water to a boil
- add salt to water, add pastina to boiling salted water and cook for 4 minutes
- drain water using a fine mesh strainer
- put pastina in bowl and add butter

Servings: 1
