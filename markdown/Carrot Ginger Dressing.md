# Carrot Ginger Dressing

## Ingredients

- `98` g rice wine vinegar
- `69` g evoo
- `200` g carrot
- `2` TBSP chopped ginger
- `2` TBSP lime juice
- `4` tsp honey
- `1.5` tsp sesame oil
- `1/4` tsp salt

## Directions

- combine all ingredients in a blender

Servings: 2
