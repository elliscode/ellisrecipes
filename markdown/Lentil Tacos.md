# Lentil Tacos

- 1 tsp canola oil
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 113 g dry red lentils
- 1 TBSP taco seasoning
- 13 oz water
- 13 g better than bouillon no chicken
- 2/3 cup salsa
Servings: 4
