# Whipped Cream

## Ingredients

- `1` cup heavy cream
- `1` tsp vanilla extract
- `1` TBSP powdered sugar

## Directions

- whip cream with whisk attachment in electric mixer until soft peaks form
- add vanilla and sugar and whip until stiff peaks form

Servings: 8
